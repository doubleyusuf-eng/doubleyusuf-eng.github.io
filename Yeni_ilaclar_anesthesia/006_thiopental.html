Reported Use

Induction of anesthesia and adjunctive agent for anesthesia; treatment of convulsive states; treatment of elevated intracranial pressure

Dosage Range

Note: Dose ranges are not intended to guide prescribing; consult local prescribing information for additional information.

IV:

Anesthesia, induction: Adults: 3 to 6 mg/kg; maintenance: 25 to 100 mg as needed.

Anticonvulsant: Adults: 75 to 250 mg/dose.

Increased intracranial pressure: Adults: 1.5 to 3.5 mg/kg/dose.

Product Availability

Product available in various countries; not currently available in the US

Dosage Forms

Solution Reconstituted, Injection, as sodium: 250 mg, 500 mg
